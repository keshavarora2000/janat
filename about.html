About Us

United Christian Church of India was instituted in 1975 with a sole aim of enhancing the quality of living and social upliftment of the economically downtrodden. Right from the inception our founder Late Mr. Samuel Wilson was definite that education would be a key contributor to the path envisioned by UCCI


“Right learning creates an array of opportunities enabling individuals to economically independent”


UCCI thereafter under the leadership of Mr. John Wilson has been toiling rigorously to establish such an environment. Education over a period of time has becoming a flourishing business and the exorbitant fees charged by such organizations have been huge deterrent in enhancing the literacy of lower income groups – UCCI is determined to break this barrier and make education affordable even to poorest of the poor.


Over the past couple of decades UCCI has been successful in establishing 6 such schools and 3 colleges in and around Telangana and Andhra Pradesh, where children from underprivileged and subjugated classes are being provided an environment to enrich their physical, intellectual and moral well-being.


Furthermore, UCCI has initiated career oriented leaning programs primarily in field of healthcare – imparting paramedical knowledge and helping scores of youngsters achieve financial independence and a secure future for themselves and their families.


***


VISION: United Christian Church of India has a simple dream of a world where no one is poor and people are able to afford what they need with certainty.


MISSION: Initiate and Sustain efforts towards the social upliftment and empowerment of the economically downtrodden by providing education, employment opportunities and health care.
